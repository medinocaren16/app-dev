 My Favorite Movie: Little Women (2019)

![Little Women Poster](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/8/8e/Little_Women_%282019%29_poster.png)

Little Women (2019) is a drama film directed by Greta Gerwig, based on the classic novel by Louisa May Alcot.  
It tells the story of the four March sisters as they navigate love, ambition, and independence during the Civil War era.

 Main Characters
- Jo March – The independent and creative writer  
- Meg March – The caring and responsible eldest sister  
- Amy March – The artistic and ambitious sister  
- Beth March – The gentle and kind sister  
- Laurie– The charming family friend
I intend to make my own way in the world.

 Why I Love It
I love Little Women because it shows the strength of women, the power of family, and the importance of following your dreams.
